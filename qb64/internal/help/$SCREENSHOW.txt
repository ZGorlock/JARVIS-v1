The '''$SCREENSHOW''' [[Metacommand]] can be used after [[$SCREENHIDE]] has been used to display the main program window throughout the program.


{{PageSyntax}}
::: $SCREENSHOW


* $SCREENSHOW can only be used AFTER [[$SCREENHIDE]] has been used!
* '''QB64 [[Metacommand]]s require that commenting or [[REM]] NOT be used!'''
