The '''_SCREENHIDE''' statement can be used to hide the main program window in a section of code.


{{PageSyntax}}
::: _SCREENHIDE


* _SCREENHIDE or [[$SCREENHIDE]] must be used before [[_SCREENSHOW]] or [[$SCREENSHOW]] can be used!
